ABOUT

This website is a portfolio of Tyla Bermingham's photography. Here you will find photographs that she has taken while on her journey around the world.

Seventeen year old Tyla loves to travel with her family. She finds happiness through capturing images of culture, landscapes and architecture. Tyla loves to express herself and feelings through art; photography and dance being her main two mediums.
